Case Converter

Convert text between different cases: uppercase, lowercase, title case, and more.

Text copied to clipboard!

How to Use

  1. Enter or paste your text in the input field
  2. Click one of the case conversion buttons to transform your text
  3. The converted text will appear in the result field
  4. Click "Copy to Clipboard" to copy the result
  5. Use "Clear" to reset both fields
