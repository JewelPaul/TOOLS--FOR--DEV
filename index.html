Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tools and services.

Are all tools free to use?
Yes, all tools on our platform are completely free to use. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone without any hidden costs or premium features.
Do I need to create an account?
No, you don't need to create an account to use our tools. All tools are instantly accessible without any registration or login required.
Are the tools mobile-friendly?
Yes, all our tools are designed to work seamlessly on mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers. The responsive design ensures a great experience across all screen sizes.
Is my data safe when using these tools?
Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. All tools run directly in your browser - we don't store or collect any of your input data. Your information never leaves your device.
Can I use these tools offline?
Currently, our tools require an internet connection to function. However, we're working on implementing offline capabilities for select tools in future updates.
How often are new tools added?
We regularly update our collection with new tools and improvements to existing ones. We actively listen to user feedback and suggestions to prioritize the development of new tools.
